Klariti flips the script on attention-economy apps. Instead of competing for your focus, it gives you and your friends the tools to protect it — through shared challenges, physical NFC tags that lock your phone, and a content layer that filters pages based on your current goal.

## What is Klariti?

Klariti is a four-platform system built around one idea: the same social mechanics that keep you hooked on the internet can be pointed at focus instead.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Intents & Challenges" icon="bullseye" href="/concepts/intents-and-challenges">
    Set a goal, invite friends, commit to a deadline. The social pressure is the feature.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gray Engine" icon="filter" href="/concepts/gray-engine">
    Context-aware content mediation that modifies webpages based on your active goal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ktags" icon="tag" href="/concepts/ktags">
    Physical NFC tags that gate focus sessions on iOS. No software bypass.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Friends" icon="users" href="/concepts/friends">
    Real accountability from people you actually want to focus with.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
